Cloud Computing Instructor

Course developer and instructor for the Cloud Computing Certificate at University of Toronto School of Continuing Studies.

Cloud computing is now integral to the digital economy. Companies value people who can understand and evaluate the design, architecture and frameworks of cloud systems. This program will help you understand different service models, including Software as a Service (SaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS) and Infrastructure as a Service (laaS). You’ll explore technology choices and development tools and get a market overview of current cloud providers. Other subjects include security and disaster recovery.
